Front Ensemble(Pit) #3

Winter Drumline and Marching Band

A typical life in pit requires more hard work and more practice. Pit people usually go up to the field first when they practice because it takes them a long time to push heavy equipments and it takes a long time to set up. Like any other section, Pit has more responsibilites for the care of their instruments. Pit instruments such as mallets instruments are delicate. Pit is a really fun section. Pit players like to goof around sometimes but they get to work and practice what they need to pratice.
