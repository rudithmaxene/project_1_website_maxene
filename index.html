Front Ensemble(Pit)

Pit, which is also called the Front Ensemble in part of Marching band and Drum line. This is the stationary percusssion section which includes mallet instruments: Bells, Xylophone, Marimba and the Vibes. It also includes The Keyboard, timpani, Synthesizer, Bass Guitars, Cymbals, Drum Kits, Congos, Bongos, Claves, Cowbells and Djembes.

The Front Ensemble,Pit, is placed by the 50 yard line and in front of the stance.

Front ensemble is also part of a thing called Winter Drumline. Winter Drumline is only for Battery and Pit. Like in Marching band, they go to compettions. Last year, Pasadena High School's Winter Drumline won first place in the Novice Division by performing "Rite of Spring" by Igor Stravinsky.
